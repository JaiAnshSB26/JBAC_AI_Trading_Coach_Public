Planner Agent

The Planner analyzes real-time market data, technical indicators (RSI, MACD, EMA), and historical patterns. It identifies trading opportunities by evaluating price trends, volume patterns, and momentum signals. The Planner creates structured trading plans with entry points, target prices, and risk assessments.

Critic Agent

The Critic acts as a risk management specialist, rigorously evaluating the Planner's recommendations. It validates trading signals against market conditions, checks for potential pitfalls, and assesses risk-reward ratios. The Critic ensures every recommendation meets strict quality and safety standards.
